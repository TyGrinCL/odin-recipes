Sirloin Steak

Description

Steak is one of the best meals that humans have created. Whether you like your steak cooked rare, medium rare, or wrong, steak is a delicious and tasty meal that should be eaten at least once per week.

Below, you will see all the ingradients needed to cook your steak, along with the steps to curate your dish.

Ingredients

  • (4) Sirloin Steaks
  • (1 tbsp) Olive Oil
  • (2 tsp) Mesquite Seasoning
  • (1/2 tsp) Salt
  • (1/2 tsp) Pepper
  • (1 tsp) Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Pat steaks dry with a paper towel.
  2. Rub steaks all over with olive oil.
  3. Season steaks with salt and pepper to taste, and mesquite seasoning.
  4. Cook steaks 3-5 minutes on each side in a skillet over medium heat OR on a grill over medium heat.
  5. Allow steaks to rest at least 5 minutes.
  6. Serve.

Conclusion
